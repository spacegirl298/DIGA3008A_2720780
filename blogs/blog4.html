Pixels, Paths & Process

I truly have been enjoying the process of making my first website. I enjoy visualizing how the website is going to look and how it is going to work. Learning HTML has had its ups and downs. I always struggle with learning something new, as I don’t like that I am not good at something when I first start it. I know this is not realistic at all, and that’s why I have just been pushing past these thoughts. After this internal battle, I have been enjoying the process of learning HTML and setting up my website. Through watching tutorials and reading through documentation, I have found the beauty in coding in Visual Studio Code. I am proud of how my website is looking so far, it obviously needs a lot more tweaking, but I am proud of how fast I picked up on certain elements. Going forward, I think I want to change my original wireframes. I like the overall design, but I created those wireframes without a goal in mind. Now that I have a goal in mind, I need to alter the wireframes to match this goal. Now that I have gone through the design process and style guide, I am more equipped to create my wireframes.
