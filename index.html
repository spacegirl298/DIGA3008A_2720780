ABOUT ME

I am a Digital Arts student at WitwatersRand University, pursuing a triple major in Interactive Media, Animation, and Game Design. Beyond Academics, I manage social media for Safe Dollar Studios and work as a part-time promotional ambassador for Volvo. Additionally, I create logos and videos for businesses on a part-time basis.

In my free time, I enjoy expressing myself through painting and drawing, often experimenting with different styles. It's a creative outlet that helps me relax and stay inspired. I love unwinding by watching series, my go-to comfort show is Friends, which I've rewatched more times than I can count.
